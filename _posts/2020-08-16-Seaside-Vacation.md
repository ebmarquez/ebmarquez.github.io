With Covid stretching into the summer, we finally had enough and needed to get out of town.  Laura saw an opportunity to rent a condo on the beach in Sea Side Oregon.  Oregon is relatively close to Seattle, about a 4 hour drive.

This summer we decided to get out of the Seattle area and visit Seaside. This was a much needed break from the day to day with CoVid.  We found it to be quite the relief to be someplace else other than our own house.  Like may of you, a change a scenery is needed every once in a while.
We didn't take a long trip, only a week long vacation but it was nice.  On most of our days we spent the evening build a fire on the beach and watching the sun set.

Here's some pictures of our trip.  This first one is a favorite of mine where it has the Laura and Julia.  As you can see Julia is not smiling, but she rarely does.
